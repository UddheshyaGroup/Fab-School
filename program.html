Learning Pathway

From Classroom to Career.

FAB School follows a practical learning journey where students develop confidence through guided training, real exposure, and career support.

01

Learn the Foundation

Build academic and technical understanding through structured classroom sessions and guided demonstrations.

02

Practice the Skills

Train inside practical labs and hotel-style environments to gain confidence in real hospitality tasks.

03

Experience Industry

Connect learning with real workplace expectations through exposure, guest interaction, and operational training.

04

Start Your Career

Receive guidance for internships, job preparation, interviews, and future hospitality career pathways.
