Common Questions

Quick Answers Before You Contact Us.

Here are a few common things students and parents usually ask before visiting or applying.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

Yes. You can contact the admissions team and schedule a visit to explore the learning spaces and facilities.

Which programs can I ask about?

You can ask about BPH, TAFE NSW course, CTEVT programs, culinary, bakery, barista, and F&B service training.

Do you provide admissions counseling?

Yes. FAB School can guide students and parents on program choice, eligibility, application steps, and career pathways.

Where is FAB School located?

FAB School is located at Chisapani, Pokhara, Nepal. You can call the school office for directions and visit timing.
