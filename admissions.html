Admission FAQs

Questions Before You Apply?

Here are some common questions students and parents ask during the admission process.

How do I apply to FAB School?

You can submit the inquiry form, call the admissions office, or visit the campus for direct counseling and application support.

Can I get help choosing a program?

Yes. The admissions team can help you compare BPH, TAFE NSW, CTEVT, and vocational courses based on your goals.

Can parents visit the campus?

Yes. Parents and students can schedule a campus visit to explore facilities and understand the learning environment.

Are vocational courses practical?

Yes. Vocational programs focus on hands-on skills in culinary, bakery, barista, and food and beverage service areas.
